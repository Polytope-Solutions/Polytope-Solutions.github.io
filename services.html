Polytope


services



Polytope is a freelance project of Daniil Koshelyuk. We specialize in combining design thinking with computational techniques such as Mixed Reality, Parametric Design, Digital Fabrication and so on. By using coding for creative applications we aim to expand possibilities of expression and communication for all stages of design from concept to fabrication or deployment in various fields, such as general design and art, archuitecture, urbanism, product design, installations, web-design and many more.

These are the main categories of our specializations:

  • Interactive Visualization
  • Parametric Design
  • Design for Digital Fabrication
  • Computational Design Courses
  • Custom Scripting
  • 3D Asset Development

If none of the described services seem to fit exactly your project idea, but you believe Polytope might be of help any way, we would gladly attend to your interests over here. Please, describe in as much details so we could do our best to find a way to collaborate.
