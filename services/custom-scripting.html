Custom Scripting



Any project in context of its target audience and target application scenario has a unique way of communication associated with it. The range of commercially available devices that allow us to finetune communication has been expanding at an unprecedented rate. And yet when considering the experience of the user, the technology has to play a secondary role allowing the user to focus on the idea behind the project itself creating an illusion of magic. This means that in many situations there is a conflicting balance that needs to be found between technical implementation and its seamless integration.

On many occasions when adapting projects to modern mediums custom development is required. On one hand, it is needed to connect elements between each other to follow a specific user experience (UX) scheme, and on the other hand, to customize and expand functionality to reach the desired form of expression. When considering such development one must take into account target audience, current hardware and software limitations for given application, best practices for scalability and durability.

Through investigation and development for many types of devices described before in past years I gained both practical experience in development and theoretical overview of possibilities and recommendations such ways of communications are entwined with.

Primary fields of application proposed by Polytope are:
 - Custom Component development for Grasshopper 3D;
 - Custom Behavior development for virtual environments in Unity3D.
These can provide either improvements for existing workflow for a company or a project, or allow to achieve nontrivial design solutions or interaction systems. Moreover, Polytope can provide technical support for custom development in design related scenarios to plan optimal UX journey, future implementations and ensure modularity, scalability and robustness of custom scripts.
