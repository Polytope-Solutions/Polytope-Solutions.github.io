Interactive Visualization



Interactive visualization encompasses use of new medium tools to convey the story behind projects to involve target audience on a deeper level and experience projects in a new and specifically tailored way.

  • Intraspace - Interior Data Visualization with Hololens, Thesis for MAA02, IAAC, 2019

Each project, depending on the focus and the idea, can be best conveyed in various ways. Mediums Polytope has experience with are:
 - Virtual Reailty (standalone or mobile);
 - Web 3D;
 - Augmented Reality (standalona or mobile);
 - Projections;
 - Sensors and actuators.

These vary in visual fidelity, types of interaction, scalability, accessibility. Some projects may require combinations of specific aspects.

Polytope can provide analysis of idea to fit interactive system and plan steps of development; organization of existing projects to work for it; setup of basic necessary systems; development of custom behaviors necessary for interaction, activation of virtual assets, and User Experiencs (UX); as well as developing details for virtual environments; or support, documentation and guidance in the development.



If you have a project in mind that could benefit from these ideas describe what you're looking for so we can start a conversation over here.
